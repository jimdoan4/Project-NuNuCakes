WE WANT TO CELEBRATE YOUR SPECIAL OCCASION

HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER

All orders need to be placed 72 hours ahead of pick up or delivery. If you have a large order for an event order needs to be placed no less than 2 weeks in advance.


HOW TO MAKE PAYMENT

Payment may be made through Cash App
At $Tamikagooden
or
Over the phone via credit or debit card


DELIVERY SERVICE

Must have a minimum of 2 dozens orders
Any Deliveries over 10 miles of Norcross, Georgia, there will be a $15 delivery charge.

OUR SPECIAL PACKAGES

Baby Package

Though we can happily assist you at either shop, we prefer you visit us at our Arroyo shop as parking is more convenient and available at no charge. Below are a few things to consider before you visit us: Do you prefer a round or square 6” cake? Do you prefer a round or square stand? Do you have a sample of your wedding color(s)? If yes, please bring them. Have you selected your flowers? Have you secured the location of your ceremony/reception? If yes, please bring the venue address and a contact name and number. Do you have a “day of” wedding coordinator? If yes, please bring their name and contact information. Other wedding details (if you have them available): What time is your ceremony? What time is your reception?
